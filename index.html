Hey, I'm Sean. I am a Full Stack Web Developer, Graphic Designer, and Musician.

I began thinking like a developer as early as high school when I taught myself how to program custom MIDI maps for my DJing equipment. In college, I applied my logical thinking skills and detail-oriented nature to pursue a Graphic Design major at Roger Williams University, where I ultimately studied UX design. I earned my B.A. in Graphic Design Communications in Dec 2015 and was hired immediately out of college as a software engineer for Gartner where my primary responsibilities were focused on the front-end. Gartner was without a doubt a struggle: after a restructure occurred internally in the company, I found myself in a position of much more responsibility and attempted to teach myself dozens of different languages and tools all at once without any proper guidance.

After over a year at Gartner, I decided it was time for me to pursue a formal education in the web development field, so I chose General Assembly's Web Development Immersive course because it taught me several of the languages and tools I was struggling to learn on my own, such as Github, JavaScript, jQuery, Ruby, Ember, and much more. Now complete, I feel confident in my abilities as a Full Stack Web Developer and UI/UX Designer and I am eager to begin a new chapter in Boston.

My goal for the future is to live in Boston while working for a small or medium sized company which will utilize all I have to offer by way of Graphic Design, UX Design, and Full Stack Web Development.
